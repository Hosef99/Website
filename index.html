Projects

Watch My Nucleus

Using Unity as the game engine and C# as the primary programming language, my team and I were able to create Watch my Nucleus in just 72 hours for the Ludum Dare 49 Game Jam. As the developer, I also composed the game's music using FL Studio 20. Despite the tight time frame, we were able to produce a physics simulation game that performed well and was well received by players. With 2939 game submissions, Watch my Nucleus was able to secure an outstanding placement of 61st place in the "Theme" category and 178th place in the "Audio" category.
